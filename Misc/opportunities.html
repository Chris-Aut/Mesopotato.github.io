Technology Opportunities

Issue with the Current Fast-food Situation

Fast-food workers deal with annoying customers, individual time-based metrics, meeting time standards for preparing meals and understaffed over workers.

Fast-food customers endure long waits, incorrect order records, wrong meal deliveries and disgruntled workers.

Current Problems with Human Workers

Customers getting undercooked food wich can lead to food poisoning in some cases.

Customers recieving wrong orders from the workers

No or lack of prior training or education required

Language barriers

Accent barriers

Advantages of AI

AI eliminates the training time for additional workers

An reduction in errors when doing menial tasks

An reduced margin of error when doing complex tasks

An overall decrease in labor intensive jobs which can be linked to chronic injuries later on in life

Steep drop towards safety concerns and workplace injury
