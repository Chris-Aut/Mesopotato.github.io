Technology Opportunities

Issue with the Current Fast-food Situation

Fast-food workers deal with annoying customers, individual time-based metrics, meeting time standards for preparing meals and understaffed over workers.

Fast-food customers endure long waits, incorrect order records, wrong meal deliveries and disgruntled workers.

Current Problems with Human Workers

Undercooked food.

Giving the wrong orders to people

No prior training or education required

Language barriers

Accent barriers

Advantages of AI

Eliminates training time for additional workers

Error reduction in menial tasks

Reduced margin of error for complex tasks

Overall decrease in labor intensive jobs which are linked to chronic injuries later in life

Steep drop towards safety concerns and workplace injury

A.I. has generally been thought of as what constitutes the future of the digital age which makes it an exciting concept to explore.
