Technology Risks

    Privacy in terms of it having a data base which registers your face, bank details, purchase times, visit logging etc which could be used for nefarious purposes

    Human bias in terms of AI learning from humans at the more advanced levels and developing similar bad behaviors that current fast food restaurant workers have

    Differing levels of service if there is some sort of system logging which favors frequently visiting customers, workers in the industry or the higher-ups

    A huge increase in job losses as AI replaces human workers in fast food outlets such as McDonalds who have roughly 10,000 employees across New Zealand
